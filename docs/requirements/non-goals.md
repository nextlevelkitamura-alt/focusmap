# Non-Goals

Last updated: 2026-05-30

Out of scope unless explicitly approved:

- Do not rewrite raw wishlist or note content as part of memo structuring.
- Do not auto-create or schedule tasks without review or a clear product rule.
- Do not mark AI decomposition as final truth without review, confidence handling, or dismissal paths.
- Do not mark MEMO-STRUCTURE as deployed without Supabase verification evidence.
- Do not mix MEMO-STRUCTURE delivery work into unrelated Codex relay changes without an explicit decision.
- Do not push, merge, or deploy from the current dirty branch without scope cleanup.
