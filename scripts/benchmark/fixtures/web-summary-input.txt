Free Plan: $0/month, 100 tasks per month, two-step Zaps only.
Limited to one trigger and one action. Includes Zapier Copilot with daily message limits.

Professional Plan: Starting at $19.99/month (billed annually). 750 tasks per month.
Premium app access included. Unlimited Zaps. Multi-step Zaps. Webhooks.

Team Plan: Starting at $69/month (billed annually). Includes 25 users.
Shared Zaps and folders. Shared app connections. SAML SSO support. Premier Support.

Enterprise Plan: Contact for pricing. Unlimited users. Advanced admin permissions
and app controls. VPC Peering. Annual task limits. Observability tools.
Technical Account Manager dedicated.
